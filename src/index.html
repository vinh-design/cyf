About Arsenal

Arsenal Football Club, founded in 1886, is one of the most successful and beloved football clubs in England. Based in North London, Arsenal has a rich history of playing beautiful, attacking football that inspires fans all over the world. The club has won multiple league titles, FA Cups, and continues to compete at the highest level. Their home ground, the Emirates Stadium, is not only a modern football arena but also a symbol of Arsenal’s ambition. Fans, known as "Gooners," are recognized worldwide for their passion, loyalty, and dedication. Supporting Arsenal is more than just watching football – it is being part of a global family united by love for the red and white.
